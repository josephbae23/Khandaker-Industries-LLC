Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2007 in Dhaka, Bangladesh, Khandaker Industries LLC was born with a singular mission: to create dignified, life-changing employment opportunities for Bangladeshi workers in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. As a Saudi-approved LLC and sister concern of the KS Group, we operate under the highest standards of recruitment ethics, regulatory compliance, and candidate welfare.

Our experienced team spans two countries, managing the full cycle from candidate sourcing and skills verification, through visa processing and travel coordination, to post-placement support — ensuring every worker arrives prepared and every employer receives reliable, qualified personnel.
